Welcome to slime making!

You are going to need a few things. We recommend you plan a trip to the office supplies section of the store to find a few ingredients. First you will want a quart of Elmers glue. Most of the time this will not be school glue -- you are going to want clear glue or any glue with sparkles or confetti or colors to reflect what you want your slime to look like.

The Other Ingredients

Make sure that you have these ingredients, and having this basic recipe will ensure slime success!

You're going to need to mix the following ingredients in the following quantities: :)

  1. 2 gallons of water
  2. 1 quart of Elmers clear glue
  3. 1 tablespoon of Borax

It looks like you are well on your way to becoming a slime master. Good job!
